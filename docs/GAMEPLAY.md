# Gameplay Rules (Current Baseline)

## Match setup
- 1 human player.
- Bot count configurable target: 1 to 3 (engine currently clamps).

## Turn structure
- Draw 1 card.
- Play up to 3 cards (play pipeline pending full implementation).
- Resolve effects.
- End turn.

## Global phase
- Every 3 completed rounds triggers global disaster phase.

## Win conditions
- Reach 50 survival points.
- Or be the last player with health above zero.
